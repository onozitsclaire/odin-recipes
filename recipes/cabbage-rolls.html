Cabbage Rolls (Golumpki)

Ingredients

  • 1 large green cabbage
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 7 large garlic cloves
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 large egg
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Chopped fresh dill

Instructions

Step 1

Boil cabbage, then using a paring knife, cut leaves

Step 2

Combine ground beef, pork, onion, garlic, grated lemon peel, egg and rice.

Step 3

In a medium pot over medium heat, add oil. Stir in grated onion and cook, then add garlic and cook until fragrant. Poor in crushed tomatoes, water, salt and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 4

Place 2 tablespoons of filling on bottom half of cabbage leaf. Fold and roll. Cover with sauce in baking pan and cook for 50-60 minutes.
